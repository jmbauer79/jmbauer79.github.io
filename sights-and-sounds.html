The bird and the reflection

On a walk during lunch, I came across a bird fighting its own reflection — a tiny, strange, almost comic scene that lingered with me after I kept walking.

A bird, its reflection, and a surprisingly useful reminder.

Teaching moment: sometimes the thing we are fighting is only a reflection of ourselves. A reminder not to become our own worst enemy.
